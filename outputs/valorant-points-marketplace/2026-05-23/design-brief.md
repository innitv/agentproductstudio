## Direction

High-energy gaming commerce landing. Inspired by official VALORANT pacing and boldness, but visually distinct.

## Visual Rules

- No Riot/VALORANT logo.
- No agent/map screenshots.
- No official red/black trade dress clone.
- Use custom neon coral, ink, off-white and electric cyan accents.
- Custom geometric card visual instead of game art.

## Components

- Header.
- Hero with offer and disclaimer.
- Pack cards.
- Safety grid.
- How-it-works timeline.
- FAQ.
- Final CTA.

## Responsive

- Desktop: hero two-column, packs three-column.
- Tablet: packs two-column.
- Mobile: single-column, sticky-safe CTA not needed in first iteration.

## Accessibility

- Buttons with clear labels.
- Region warnings are text, not color-only.
- Strong contrast.
- No text over busy imagery.
