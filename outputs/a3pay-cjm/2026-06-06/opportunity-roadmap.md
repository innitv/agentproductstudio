## Recommended 12-24 Month Roadmap

### 0-6 месяцев

- MVP: `payment request by phone`, verified recipient, method choice, receipt.
- Integrations: СБП/link payment, card fallback through PSP, basic merchant dashboard.
- Vertical pilots: ЖКХ/телеком bills and small service merchants.

### 6-12 месяцев

- Unified bill inbox: recurring payments, reminders, subscription control.
- Smart checkout SDK for e-commerce: card/SБП/BNPL routing.
- Family/shared payments: pay for another person with transparent receipt.

### 12-18 месяцев

- Auto payment hub: штрафы, ОСАГО, сервис, парковка, налоги.
- Travel payment bundle: booking, split, insurance, refund tracker.
- Partner BNPL/credit marketplace with transparent cost display.

### 18-24 месяца

- Property payment checklist as trust product, not mass MVP.
- Digital ruble acceptance rail readiness.
- Advanced reconciliation for merchants and document-linked payments.
