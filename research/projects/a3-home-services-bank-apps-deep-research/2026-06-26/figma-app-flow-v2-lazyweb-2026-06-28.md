## Design Decisions

- P0 flow is centered on `оплатить ЖКУ -> понять статус поставщика -> исправить долг`, not on a catalog of A3 capabilities.
- Secondary scenarios are entry points from the object screen, not equal top-level pages.
- The board uses a restrained mobile banking style: white/light-gray surfaces, black primary CTA, compact source/status chips, dense cards and bottom navigation.
- A3 value is shown as data normalization and status routing: address, bill, provider, receipt, responsible participant.
- Strong Lazyweb evidence was used for banking/payment/maintenance patterns; weak Lazyweb results for meters/family were explicitly treated as adjacent only.

## Remaining Risks

- This is a product-specific concept board, not a production Alfa/T-Bank design-system implementation.
- A3 endpoint/API names remain `needs_validation`; the UI avoids promising exact backend behavior.
- Meter readings and family access had weaker Lazyweb coverage, so they remain secondary screens and should be validated with real product references later.
